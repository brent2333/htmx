About This Site

What you're looking at is built on ExpressJS with a Potgresql DB and HTMX. After years of fatigue dealing with Angular and NextJS Front-Ends; never seeming to make truly efficient use my time, the user's time or the company's budget, I began to desire the experience of working on something that felt reasonable to me.

After stumbling upon Hypermedia Systems, it was clearer to me than ever what my fatigue was all about.
